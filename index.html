About

Hidden Food Club - Craft Cocktails & Hidden Flavors

At Hidden Food Club, our story is rooted in bold flavor, crafted drinks, and unforgettable atmosphere in the heart of North Beach, Miami Beach. Tucked around a lush poolside oasis just steps from the ocean, Hidden is more than a restaurant it is an escape.

Led by Chef Xavier Nicolas Amarilla, our menu brings together Argentine soul and global inspiration, from signature empanadas and octopus carpaccio to standout pastas and curated cocktails.

Whether you join us for a laid-back brunch, a vibrant happy hour, or a music-filled night, every detail is designed to feel intentional, elevated, and worth discovering.
